## 2. Business Description
Meta Platforms, Inc. is a global technology company focused on connecting people through its diverse portfolio of products and services. Its core operations are divided into two main segments: Family of Apps (FoA) and Reality Labs (RL). The FoA segment includes widely used platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, WhatsApp, and Threads, which enable billions of users to connect, share, and communicate across various devices. This segment generates substantial revenue primarily through advertising.

The Reality Labs (RL) segment is Meta's ambitious bet on the future of immersive digital experiences. It encompasses virtual reality (VR) and augmented reality (AR) products, including Meta Quest VR headsets, consumer hardware, software, and content designed to foster social connections in the metaverse. Additionally, RL is developing wearables such as AI glasses (e.g., Ray Ban Meta) and advanced input devices like the Meta Neural Band, aiming to integrate AI and immersive technology into daily life. Meta also engages in strategic collaborations with major tech firms like Microsoft, NVIDIA, and OpenAI to further its technological advancements.
